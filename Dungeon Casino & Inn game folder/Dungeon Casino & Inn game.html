CAN YOU CLEAR THE DUNGEON?

Be careful of Thormir the Dragon!

Game Rules: 2+ Players have to rely on 2d6 dice after Thormir stole the rest of your dice and retreated into his Dungeon.

Take turns rolling your dice and see if you have the might to clear Thormir's Dungeon!

1. Role 2 or 4; you find a Mimic acting as die, skip your turn!
2. Role 3 or 5; you find your die, extra turn!
3. Role a double 5; you'll blow a hole through the dungeon, you crazy beast!
4. Role 7 or 11; YOU CLEAR THE DUNGEON! CONGRATS EXPLORER!
5. Role a 12; YOU SLAY THORMIR AND GATHER HIS RICHES! ENJOY YOUR FORTUNE!
6. Role a 0; you'll be seen as the unluckiest person alive- or should I say dead, since Thormir will swoop in and eat you!
7. Role any other number; you continue to the next part of the dungeon.
